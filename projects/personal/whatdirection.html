WhatDirection

What is it?

WhatDirection is a simple app to get the "as the crow flies" direction to a destination. First, you pick a destination. Then, when you face that direction, the phone will vibrate. There is a sensitivity meter to widen or shorten the range.

How does it work?

The app gathers two bearing coordinates.

The app gets your current location's longitude and latitude. When you select a destination, the app retrieves the the longitude and latitude of the destination. The app uses the haversine formula to convert both pairs of coordinates into a bearing.

The app then uses the phone's magnetometer to determine magnetic north in relation to your phone. It converts the results of the magnetometer into a bearing.

The app then will buzz when the bearings are close, according to your sensitivity. A stronger sensitivity simply shortens the window in which the two bearings are considered close.
